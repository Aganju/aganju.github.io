About Me

I am a software engineer with a focus in web development using Ruby on Rails, React and Redux. I've been fascinated by computers ever since my first experience using one at 10 years old in Nigeria. As a result I chose to major in Computer Science at the New Jersey Institute of Technology from which I graduated in 2017. My interest in web development really blossomed into a passion when I took the opportunity to explore it as part of my Capstone Project where I used a JavaScript library for the first time. I've since seized the opportunity to continue honing my skill in this area and I am now seeking a company where I can put my talents to work in solving challenging problems with real world impact.
